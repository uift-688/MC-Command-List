基本のコマンド
TP: 
/tp <Position: 'x y z'>
/tp <Selector: Any> <Position: 'x y z'>
/tp <Selector: Any> <Selector: Any>
Give: 
/give <Selector: Selector> <Item-ID: String> <Item-Qty: Integer>
SetBlock: 
/setblock <Position: 'x y z'> <Block-ID: String> [Block-Value: Integer] [Operation: replace | keep | destroy]
Weather: 
/weather <Weather-Type: clear | rain | thunder> [Time: Integer]
Kill: 
/kill <Selector: Selector>
Effect: 
/effect give <Selector: Selector> <Effect-ID: String> [Time: Integer | infinity] [Strength: Integer]
/effect clear <Selector: Selector> <Effect-ID: String>
Say: 
 /say [Say-Text: Any]
GameMode: 
 /gamemode  [Selector: Selector]
Server: Multiverse-Core
